RS-40
v1.0.0
Preamp Emulation Plugin

Free analog preamp emulation inspired by the Ashly SC-40 instrument preamp. Features 3-band semi-parametric EQ with sweepable frequency controls and optional diode saturation. Designed for bass guitar.

The SC-40 is notably part of the signal chain Geddy Lee used to record his bass tone on Rush's Moving Pictures.
